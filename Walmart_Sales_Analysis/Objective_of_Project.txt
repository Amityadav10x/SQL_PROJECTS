The primary objective of this project is to perform a comprehensive analysis of D-Mart sales data to extract valuable insights and support data-driven decision-making. By leveraging SQL, the project aims to:

Understand sales trends and patterns across different time periods and locations.
Identify the most popular products and payment methods.
Analyze customer behavior and demographics to tailor marketing strategies.
Evaluate the performance of various branches and product lines.
Assess the impact of promotional activities and optimize inventory management.
Provide actionable insights that can enhance operational efficiency and increase overall revenue.
